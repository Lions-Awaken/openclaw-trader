What is OpenClaw Trader?

OpenClaw Trader is an autonomous swing trading agent that runs 24/7 on a NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano. It monitors the US equity market, identifies high-probability setups using a multi-signal hybrid strategy, sizes positions with ATR-based risk management, executes trades via Alpaca Markets, and learns from every outcome through a RAG memory system.

The agent is aggressive but bounded — it hunts for real edge while enforcing hard risk limits: 5% risk per trade, max 3 concurrent positions, circuit breakers for consecutive losses, weekly drawdowns, and VIX spikes. When in doubt, it does nothing. Cash is a position.

The 6-Signal System

A trade requires minimum 4 of 6 signals aligned before entry:

Signal 1 — Trend (30-35%): Price above 21 EMA, 21 EMA above 50 EMA, price above 200 SMA
Signal 2 — Momentum (20-25%): RSI(14) between 35-65 or Williams %R crossing above -80
Signal 3 — Volume (15%): OBV trending up 3+ days, volume 1.5x 20-day average on breakouts
Signal 4 — Fundamental Catalyst (15%): Earnings beat, analyst upgrade, CapEx news
Signal 5 — Sentiment (10-15%): Finnhub + Perplexity news score > +0.3 (below -0.5 is a hard veto)
Signal 6 — Flow (optional): Unusual options activity, insider buying, congressional trades

Technology Stack

Jetson Orin Nano
Always-on ARM edge computer. 8GB RAM, GPU-accelerated inference. Runs the entire trading pipeline locally.
OpenClaw Runtime
AI agent daemon with cron scheduling, MCP tool integration, and persistent memory. Orchestrates all trading operations.
Qwen 2.5 3B (Ollama)
Local LLM for fast screening, regime checks, and midday portfolio reviews. Free, no API calls, instant inference on Jetson GPU.
Claude Sonnet 4.6 (Anthropic)
Heavy reasoning model for pre-market scans, signal evaluation, trade decisions, and nightly reflections. Used when precision matters.
Alpaca Markets + MCP Server
Commission-free trading API with 43 MCP tool endpoints. Paper and live trading, fractional shares, real-time market data.
Supabase pgvector
PostgreSQL with vector embeddings for RAG memory. Every trade decision is embedded and searchable — the agent learns from similar past setups.
Finnhub + Perplexity
Real-time market news, sentiment scoring, insider trading data, and AI-powered research synthesis.
Telegram Bot
Trade approval flow for positions over $100. Sends alerts, P&L updates, and end-of-day summaries directly to your phone.

Daily Schedule (Eastern Time)

9:35 AM — Market Open
Check all open positions against current prices. Execute pending exits for stop losses or profit targets hit at open.
12:30 PM — Midday Check
Review positions vs stop/target levels. Check for regime shifts. Scan sentiment for news since morning.
4:05 PM — Market Close
Time stop enforcement (day 10 = forced exit). Update position log. Nightly reflection — what worked, what didn't, pattern recognition.
Sunday 6 PM — Weekly Maintenance
Watchlist cleanup, new candidate screening, sector news review, weekly P&L summary to Telegram.

Risk Management

The agent enforces these rules without exception:

  • Max risk per trade: 5% of account ($25 on a $500 base)
  • Position sizing: ATR-based stops determine share count (never manual)
  • Stop loss: 1.5x ATR below entry — never moved down, only up
  • Max concurrent positions: 3
  • Time stop: Force exit after 10 trading days regardless of P&L
  • Take profit ladder: 40% at 1.5R, 40% at 2.5R, 20% trailing at 1x ATR

Circuit Breakers

  • 3 consecutive losses → 24hr pause + 50% position size for next 5 trades
  • Weekly loss ≥ 8% → Halt until Monday
  • Account drawdown ≥ 15% → Full halt, requires manual approval
  • VIX > 28 → No new entries
